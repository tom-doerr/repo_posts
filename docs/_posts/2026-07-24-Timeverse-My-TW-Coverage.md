# [Timeverse/My-TW-Coverage](https://github.com/Timeverse/My-TW-Coverage)

A structured database covering 1,735 Taiwan-listed companies across 99 industries. It utilizes a knowledge graph with 4,900-plus wikilinks to map supply chains, enabling searches like identifying 207 companies in Apple's supply chain or tracing TSMC's CoWoS partners.
