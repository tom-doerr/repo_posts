# [batear-io/batear](https://github.com/batear-io/batear)

Batear is an ultra-low-cost acoustic drone detector running on the ESP32-S3 microcontroller. It operates entirely at the edge with no cloud subscription or internet connection. Alerts can be sent via encrypted LoRa or wired Ethernet PoE.
