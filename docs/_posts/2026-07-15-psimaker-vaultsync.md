# [psimaker/vaultsync](https://github.com/psimaker/vaultsync)

Self-hosted Obsidian vault sync for iPhone and iPad. Syncs peer-to-peer over Syncthing straight into Obsidian's iOS sandbox, keeping notes local without a cloud account.
